About Me

Frontend Developer!

Hi, I'm Shweta Chandra 🙋‍♀️ — a B.Sc. Biotechnology with IT student at Netaji Subhas University, Jamshedpur 🧬💻. I'm passionate about web development and love creating websites that are both functional and visually appealing 🌐✨. I've already made several projects using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I'm always eager to learn more.

🔽 Read more about my journey

As I continued my studies in Biotechnology 🧬, I discovered a strong interest in web development 💻. What started as curiosity soon turned into passion — I loved learning how websites work and how I could build something from scratch that people can actually use 🌐✨. Since then, I've completed several projects using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript 🧑‍💻, which helped me understand how to make responsive and interactive websites. To gain real-world experience, I joined a 2-month internship with Unified Mentor 💼 on May 1st. Here, I'm working on front-end tasks, writing clean code, and learning how to meet deadlines and collaborate with a team 🤝⌛. After the internship, I plan to dive into backend development 🛠️, exploring tools like Node.js and MongoDB. My goal is to become a full-stack developer 🚀 — someone who can build entire websites and web apps from front to back. Even though my academic background is in Biotechnology, I believe combining my knowledge in science 🔬 with my tech skills opens up amazing opportunities — especially in building educational platforms, health-related web apps, or research tools that make a real difference. I enjoy solving problems 🧠, building useful things 🏗️, and learning something new every day 📚. I'm excited to keep growing in this field and see where this journey takes me 🌟. Let's connect and create something impactful together! 🙌
